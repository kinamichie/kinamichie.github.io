Anchorage, Alaska Temple

The Anchorage, Alaska temple is the first and only temple built in Alaska. It was dedicated on January 9-10, 1999 by Gordon B. Hinckley. It was one of the smaller temples with only one endowment room. After being enlarged it was rededicated on February 8, 2004. The Celestial Room has a beautiful 700-pound chandelier featuring thousands of Hungarian crystals and 140 lights. This temple is located in southern Anchorage just off the Seward Highway. It is the northernmost temple of the Church. There are no services available there so if you are visiting from out of town, make sure you bring your own temple clothing.
